Chengdu Shishi Tianfu High School is a public boarding middle school directly under the management committee of Chengdu High-tech Zone. Chengdu Shishi Tianfu Middle School covers an area of 160 acres, with a construction area of 6,6832.7 square meters. It has a scientifically and rationally arranged gymnasium and a 400-meter standardized football stadium; a science and technology complex building with recording and broadcasting classrooms, film and television projection rooms, psychology rooms, music classrooms, and fully equipped physical and chemical laboratory.
